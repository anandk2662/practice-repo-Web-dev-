Monkey D.Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy is the cheerful and fearless captain of the Straw Hat Pirates. After eating the Gum-Gum Fruit, his body gained rubber powers, making him a creative and unpredictable fighter. With an unshakable will and a dream to become the Pirate King, Luffy inspires his crew and allies through his loyalty, courage, and determination.
